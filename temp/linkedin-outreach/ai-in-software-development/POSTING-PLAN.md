## Recovery Tactics (If Posts Flop)

### If engagement is low:
1. Don't delete (looks bad)
2. Engage heavily with others' content
3. Wait 24 hours before next post
4. Try same topic with different hook

### Safe recovery posts:
- Poll (always gets engagement)
- Story post (relatable)
- Hot take (drives comments)

---

## Content Tone Guidelines

### DO:
- Share genuine experiences (wins AND failures)
- Acknowledge uncertainty
- Ask questions and invite discussion
- Be specific (tool names, real numbers)
- Admit when AI doesn't work

### DON'T:
- Hype AI as silver bullet
- Dismiss AI skeptics
- Claim to have all answers
- Be preachy or condescending
- Only share successes
