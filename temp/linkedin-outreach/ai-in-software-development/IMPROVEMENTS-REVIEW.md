# Campaign Review & Improvements
## AI in Software Development - 12-Week LinkedIn Campaign

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

After thorough review, the campaign has strong foundations but needs adjustments to prevent:
1. Content fatigue from repetitive themes
2. Audience disengagement from over-used flagship stats
3. Reduced credibility from too-similar carousels

### Issue #6: "Vibe Coding" Post Risk ⚠️ REVIEW NEEDED

**Post #10:** "I vibe coded a production service at NVIDIA"

**Risks:**
- Associates you with controversial term
- May alienate skeptics
- Could seem defensive

**Options:**
1. **Keep:** If you want to own the term
2. **Soften:** "Call it what you want - here's what quality looks like"
3. **Replace:** Focus on the quality metrics without the label

**Recommendation:** Keep but monitor comments. Have backup ready.
