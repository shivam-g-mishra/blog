## FIRST COMMENT

The documents we share on Day 1:

1. **AI Usage Guidelines**
   - When to use AI (boilerplate, tests, docs)
   - When NOT to use AI (security-critical, complex logic first pass)
   - Review requirements

2. **Prompt Library**
   - Templates that work for us
   - Context-setting patterns
   - Common mistakes to avoid

3. **Our Standards**
   - Coding conventions
   - Testing requirements
   - What "done" looks like
